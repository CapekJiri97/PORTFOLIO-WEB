Marketing Mix Analysis: Prusa Research

Tool: Power BI
Source: Survey (B2C)
Focus: 4P & Loyalty

About this project:
The main goal of this survey was to understand the current market position of Prusa Research. I aimed to define the typical customer persona, identify key decision-making parameters (Speed, Quality, Price), and analyze how the 4P marketing mix is perceived by Prusa owners compared to users of competing brands.

1. The Data Pipeline

I managed the whole data pipeline from collection to visualization. This was an end-to-end analytics project:

Data Collection

Designed a survey to analyze market trends, brand awareness sources (Social media, Events), and customer loyalty.

Cleaning (Excel)

Processed raw data, categorized open-ended answers, and segmented users into "Prusa Owners" vs. "Competitors".

Viz (Power BI)

Built an interactive dashboard to visualize the 4P perception and loyalty metrics across different user segments.

2. Key Market Insights

The analysis of the survey data revealed clear trends regarding the customer base and brand strength:

Customer Persona

The typical customer is a Technical Enthusiast (Male, 26-45, HS/Uni education) who purchases 3D printing goods ~3x/month. This confirms Prusa's strong B2C position.

High Loyalty

Prusa boasts exceptional loyalty (>50% retention) compared to competitors (>66% switchers). The main driver is Long-term Quality (42%), followed by Community and Ecosystem.

Perception Gap

Prusa owners rate the brand's Quality and Price significantly higher than non-owners. Interestingly, Bambu Lab owners are the most critical regarding Prusa's price/performance ratio.

Personal Reflection

This project taught me that data interpretation is as important as visualization. The survey revealed that while competitors may win on specs, Prusa's retention is driven by long-term trust. Translating these qualitative insights into a Power BI story was the key challenge and learning outcome.
