Marketing Mix Analysis: Prusa Research

Tool: Power BI
Source: Survey Data
Focus: Brand Loyalty
View Analysis PDF

About this project:
This project originated as a university group assignment focused on analyzing the market position of Prusa Research. I saw this as the perfect opportunity to get hands-on with Power BI for the first time, transforming our raw survey data into an interactive story focused on loyalty and pricing. My goal was to move beyond theory and work with real data to understand why customers choose Prusa over emerging competitors.

1. The Data Pipeline

I managed the whole data pipeline from collection to visualization. This was an end-to-end analytics project:

Data Collection

Designed a survey and distributed it to 3D printing communities. Gathered responses from hobbyists and pros.

Cleaning (Excel)

The raw data was messy. Used Excel to clean inconsistencies, categorize open-ended answers, and structure it.

Visualisation (PBI)

Built an interactive dashboard to visualize trends, allowing filtering by user type (Owners vs. Non-owners).

2. Key Market Insights

The analysis of survey data revealed clear trends regarding brand perception and market shifts:

Brand Loyalty

Prusa users exhibit extremely high loyalty due to the open-source ecosystem and exceptional customer support, justifying the higher price point.

The Bambu Threat

There is a visible shift of users considering Bambu Lab. The data shows they are winning on "speed" and "convenience," identifying a key strategic threat.

Price Sensitivity

While Creality dominates the budget sector, Prusa owners are proven to be less price-sensitive if the quality and reliability are maintained.

Personal Reflection

This was my first end-to-end data project. The biggest challenge wasn't the visualization itself, but interpreting the data – turning rows in Excel into a coherent story about brand loyalty. It taught me that a dashboard doesn't need to be complex to provide valuable business insights.
