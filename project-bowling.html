Bowling Performance Analytics

A "passion project" applying advanced data analytics to sports. Moving beyond simple averages to track consistency, mental endurance, and current form using custom KPIs.

View Dashboard

1. The Challenge: Beyond the Average

Standard bowling statistics (average score) often mask the true nature of a player's performance. A player with a 150 average might be wildly inconsistent, while another is steady. I wanted to answer questions like:

  • Who performs better under pressure (later stages)?
  • Is a player's current form trending up or down?
  • Who is the most consistent player regardless of score?

2. Advanced DAX Engineering

I engineered several custom measures using functions like CALCULATE, FILTER, UNION, and SUMMARIZE.

1. Player Consistency (Coefficient of Variation):
Calculated the relative standard deviation to measure stability. Helps identify "Wildcards" vs. "Steady Eddies".
2. Current Form (Weighted Moving Average):
Assigns higher weights to recent games (50% last, 33% previous, 17% before) to track immediate trends.

Inspired by Gartner's business charts, I created a scatter plot correlating "Form" (Y-axis) vs. "Consistency" (X-axis). This allows for instant player segmentation.
